Agent & Chunk based City Generation


This project, developed for my MSc in Game Engineering, tackles the challenge of generating large and performant cities in real time. The goal was to research and develop chunk-based algorithms capable of producing infinite cities.

Key Features

Using Unity and C#, I designed and implemented three algorithms to explore the different trade-offs between performance and visuals.

  • Agent-Based Generator: A conventional road generator that simulates organic growth to serve as a baseline for visual quality, generating natural and meandering road networks.

  • Grid-Based Chunk Generator: Generates a road network aligned to a grid and within individual chunks, optimised for speed.

  • Voronoi-Based Chunk Generator: A modified algorithm of the previous generator, using Voronoi cells to randomise the shape of each chunk and produce more natural, organic cells.

A pipeline was created to translate these 2D road networks into 3D geometry, including roads and extruded building footprints.
